Description

With meat, cheese, pasta, and tomato sauce being the pillars of the dish, there is no wrong way to make a lasagna. Fortunately, there is a near-perfect way. This is a time-consuming recipe where each step will contribute towards a much beloved lasagna for everyone to try! Follow along to the best of your abilities and prepare to be amazed.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the instructions.
  5. Cover with foil and bank
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
  7. Layer: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, mozzarella slices, meat sauce, parmesan cheese, repeat
