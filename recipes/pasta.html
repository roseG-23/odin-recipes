Pesto Tortellini

Home

Description

With minimal ingredients, this pasta dish makes for a great meal to have at the end of a long day. The pesto puts a spin on the tried-and-true combination of pasta and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Tri-color cheese tortellini
  • Pesto
  • Chopped prosciutto
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients and preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit or 180 degrees celsius
  2. Boil pasta in salted water for three minutes, then drain
  3. Mix the tortellini in a bowl with the rest of the ingredients
  4. Pour the mixed pasta into a baking dish and sprinkle mozzarella over the top
  5. Bake in oven for about 10 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and golden
