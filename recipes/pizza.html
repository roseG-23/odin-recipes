Lavash Pizza

Home

Description

Lavash pizza is a quick and easy flatbread meal that can be customized with your preferred ingredients. The thin crust makes for a nice bite and your favorite toppings guarantee it to be a hit!

Ingredients

  • Lavash flatbread
  • Pizza sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit or 180 degrees celsius, spray one side of the lavash with nonstick spray
  2. Bake the lavash on the oven rack for two minutes
  3. Remove the lavash, place on a baking tray, and spread two to three tablespoons of sauce on each lavash
  4. Sprinkle two ounces of mozzarella cheese on each lavash, top with pepperoni
  5. Bake the pizza for 15 minutes or until the cheese is melted and golden
